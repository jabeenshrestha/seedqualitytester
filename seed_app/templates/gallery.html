Maize is a general crop that is cultivated extensively during summer (April - August) in the Terai and the Hilly region of Nepal. As it is the most significant crop in the context of Nepal, the farmer and the general people must be aware about all the aspects of Maize seed i.e. the type of maize seed to sow, the time for the maize plant to grow completely, location and region to be cultivated, etc. As per the research made on 2013 by National Maize Research Program, Rampur, Chitwan under Nepal Agricultural Research Council, following fruitful statiscics can be obtained:

3 Nutan (KH-101), F1 2010 6.5-8 90-92 Mid Hill Mid Dev. Region-summer season
